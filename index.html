Super Bowl Recap

Hard Work Pays Off

Despite being a well respected head coach for over 20 years, Andy Reid struggled to win a Super Bowl trophy. After persistent hard work, he finally benefitted from his team's comeback and kissed the sweet bliss of the Lombardi trophy. Now, no one can say that there is that one little blemish on his all time career.
